The Great Wall of China is a Historical Landmark, that attracts 10's of Millions of People a Year. Its creation started all the way back in 220 B.C.E, by a man named Qin Shi Huang. At the time Qin Shi Huang was the first and only emperor to ever exist in China. The Great Wall of China was one of the many things that is important in our current day world, that Qin Shi Huang made.
